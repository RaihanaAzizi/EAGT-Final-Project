Statistics and Facts about Child Labor

Explor to the facts and Statistics about child labor around the world . Learn more about the scope , types , and effects of child labor .

Global Statistics

According to the International Labor Organization(ILO) , more than 150 million children are engaged in child labor worldwide .These childrent are under the age 14 and are subjected to harsh working conditiona in various industries

Number of children in Child Labor

Currently , around 150 million children are involved in child Labor globally , with the highest rates seen in developing countries .

Child Labor by Region

Child labor is most prevalent in Asia , Africa , and parts of Latin America . In these regions , children are commonly found working in agriculture , mining , and factories .
